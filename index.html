      • About

      Obsessive Compulsive Disordered known as oCD is a highly simplified texture pack that gives you 
      simple cartoony feel/look, it was originally created by FVDisco in 16x16 resolutions for Java Edition
      but later people like "Sydney600MC" recreated it in 32x32, 64x64 and even 128x128 resolutions which
      add more details to the textures.

      • Info

      The last official update was for 1.8 java edition. We never seen any official update after that. 
      So people like Sydney, peter and many others decided to update the pack. During this Sydney recreated
      the pack in 32x32 and 128x128 which add hell of the details in it. 

      Just like that i have also recreated the pack in 32x32 resolution for latest minecraft bedrock versions.
      Its fully updated for the current version of the game with all new blocks, entities, fonts and even gui is 
      in ocd.

      Even though i am not the original creator of the ocd pack sous i cant copyright it and FVDisco never did that
      too but since i recreated the pack which honestly not that easy and also took really long time and hard 
      work to make. Knowing that i won't accept that anyone steal my work either to use it for your own pack or
      Republish it without giving me a credit.
